Hardware to Play ROCm

ROCm Platform supports two generations of Graphics Core Next GPU's.

Hawaii: FirePro W9100(16GB), S9150(16 GB), S9170(32 GB) large memory footprint with strong 1/2 rate double precision performance at 2.5 TFlops with a memory bandwidth of 320 GB/s.

Fiji: Radeon R9 Nano and FirePro S9300 x2 with revolutionary HBM memories with 512 GB/s per ASIC. Currently the highest efficiency of 46 GFLOPS/watt. R9 Nano is 8 Tflops and S9300x2 support solid 14.9 Tflops of single precision performance to attack Machine Learning, Molecular Dynamics, Energy, and Signal Processing Problems

CPU Support: ROCm requires CPU's with PCIe Gen3 Atomics supports such as the Intel Xeon E5 v3 or newer Xeon processors. It will also support consumer class CPU such as the Haswell class Intel Core CPU processors or newer. We know your going to ask, yes ROCm is optimized to pull all the rich new capabilities out of the new AMD Zen based processors.
